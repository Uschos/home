The UNIQVERSITY© Life

Themed 2018 "Discover, Change, Impact”,

The UNIQVERSITY© Life is a student leader summit jointly organized by U-Schos and UCSI University.  This summit aims to inculcate leadership values among young student leaders from various secondary schools around Malaysia. Various activities and sessions allow student leaders to discover their talents and potential, challenge their body and mind, and explore on ideas that bring positive change and impact to the society.
